Welcome to EcoSphere Urban Farm & Sanctuary

At the heart of Seattle, we're revolutionizing urban agriculture with sustainable practices and compassionate animal care.

Learn more about our dedication to sustainable farming, ethical animal care, and fresh, locally grown produce in this short video.

      

Integrated Farming Systems

Hydroponic Marvels

Our state-of-the-art hydroponic systems produce lush, nutrient-rich greens year-round. Witness towers of lettuce and herbs thriving without soil, maximizing space and minimizing water usage.

Aquaponic Synergy

Experience the harmony of fish and plants in our aquaponic setup. Tilapia provide nutrients for thriving vegetable beds, creating a closed-loop system that exemplifies efficiency and sustainability.

Traditional Wisdom

We honor time-tested farming methods in our outdoor plots. Seasonal vegetables and heritage crops flourish in carefully tended soil, proving that traditional techniques have a place in modern urban farming.

Ethical Animal Care

Happy Hens, Healthy Eggs

Our free-range chickens enjoy spacious coops and organic feed, producing the most delicious, golden-yolked eggs you've ever tasted.

Bee Haven

Our apiary is a sanctuary for these vital pollinators. Our bees forage on diverse urban flora, creating unique, flavorful honey while supporting local ecosystems.

Rescued Farm Friends

Meet our gentle goats and sheep, rescued from industrial farms. They now live peacefully in our sanctuary, serving as ambassadors for compassionate animal care.

Join Our Sustainable Journey

At EcoSphere, we're more than a farm – we're a community dedicated to sustainable living and ethical food production.
